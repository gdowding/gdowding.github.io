1 Why blog with emacs org?

I do most of my work in emacs and prefer to do any writing in org-mode. It fits my way of working with source and building a product. I'm already taking lots of notes in emacs org-mode. I am hopeful (new year and all) that I can convert these to actual blogs and articles. Finally, since it is logargely based on elisp, it can all be customized. In practice, I haven't spent much time doing any serious elisp customization. Again, I'm hopefull that I can spend some time automating common programming and writing tasks.

Also leverages git.
